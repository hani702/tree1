Inexpensive Tree Care


There are many different types of tree services and the costs can vary greatly. Small trees will cost around $500 to $600 to remove, while large trees will cost between $800 and $1,700. For trees over 80 feet tall, you will likely pay around $1,500 to $3,000. Aside from the cost of the tree itself, you will also need to pay for stump removal and clean-up.



Poplar trees, for example, can grow to 90 to 115 feet and have extensive roots. A poplar tree removal can cost around $1500 to $2,000.
